Chefcitos: Designing a Seamless Culinary Experience

Led the UX/UI design and contributed to the front-end development of a recipe platform that allows users to explore, share, and discover recipes, making cooking more accessible and enjoyable.

Year
2025
Timeline
2 months
Tools and Technologies
Figma, Angular, Electron, Tailwind, Supabase
About Chefcitos

Through user research, we found that many people struggle to find recipes that match their preferences and keep track of favorites, making meal planning overwhelming. Chefcitos addresses this by encouraging recipe sharing and building a supportive community that makes cooking more enjoyable and sustainable.

My Role

As design lead, I shaped the product from concept to implementation by defining the visual identity, designing key flows and reusable components, and creating a comprehensive design system.

In addition to design, I collaborated on the front-end development by building components in Angular, implementing selected screens, and supporting testing and iteration to ensure design fidelity and usability.

The Outcome

The final product delivered a cohesive, friendly experience that simplified recipe discovery and meal planning. Users could easily explore content, save favorites, and feel inspired to cook more confidently, while the design system provided a strong foundation for ongoing development.
