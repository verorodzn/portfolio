Overview

As the product evolved, our existing components couldn’t keep up. The design system grew heavy and cluttered, while limited documentation led to confusion and rework. It became clear we needed a unified, scalable design system to support both teams and streamline collaboration.

My Role

As the UX Lead I...

Led the redesign and cleanup of the existing component library

Created clear documentation and guidelines to support cross-team alignment

Architecture

Its foundation combined modern design principles and accessibility standards to ensure a cohesive experience throughout the product.

Built using Atomic Design

Based on Material Design guidelines

Designed to be responsive and meet WCAG AA accessibility standards

Implemented design tokens and shared styles for consistency and easy updates

Organized Figma libraries for clarity, reusability, and smooth team collaboration
